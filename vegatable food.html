Vegetable Dishes

Vegetable dishes are meals prepared mainly from plant-based ingredients like leafy greens, root vegetables, and legumes. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber, making them essential for a balanced diet. These dishes are often lighter, easier to digest, and suitable for people of all ages.

Each type provides different flavors and nutrient combinations, supporting overall health. Because they are low in fat and high in antioxidants, vegetable dishes help maintain good digestion and boost immunity.
